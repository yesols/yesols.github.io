Hello!

My name is Yesol (pronounced like yes-all), a PhD student in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, a registered nurse in oncology, and a hobby artist who loves illustrations in ink.

My academic interests oscillated between basic science and clinical nursing throughout my life; the desire to be a scientist finally won over, and my husband and I moved to beautiful Idaho with our cat to pursue our studies.

I enjoy thinking about fundamental biological processes (i.e. how DNA and RNA work, how proteins get made, etc) in the context of biochemical and physical principles.

Check out my pages for more info and feel free to contact me if you have any questions or comments.

Cheers!
